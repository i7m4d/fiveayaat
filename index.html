Five Ayaat Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Five Ayaat (the app) is built to respect your privacy. The app has no user accounts, works fully offline, and does not track you. This policy explains the limited information the app uses and where it goes.

Information the app uses

  • Location. If you grant permission, the app reads your device location to calculate accurate prayer times for your city. Your coordinates, and the city or country resolved from them, are stored only on your device. They are never sent to us or to any server. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in the iOS Settings app, and the app will then let you choose your location manually.
  • Notifications. If you allow notifications, the app schedules reminders locally on your device. No notification information ever leaves your device.
  • Reading activity. Your streak, reading history, and progress are stored only on your device.

Purchases

If you subscribe to Five Ayaat Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat, our subscription provider. To verify and restore your subscription, RevenueCat receives a randomly generated app user identifier along with basic purchase and device information. We never receive your name, your email address, or your payment details, because Apple handles all payment information. For more detail, please read the RevenueCat Privacy Policy and the Apple Privacy Policy.

What we do not do

  • We do not collect or store your information on our servers.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with anyone.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date shown above and publish the revised version at this address.
